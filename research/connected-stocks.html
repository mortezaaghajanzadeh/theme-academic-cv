Connected Stocks via Business Groups

Morteza Aghajanzadeh, Mahdi Heidari, and Mahdi Mohseni

Abstract

Using daily ownership data from Iran, we show that both direct and indirect common ownership increase stock-return comovement, with business-group affiliation playing the dominant role. The results highlight coordinated trading patterns within groups as a key mechanism linking ownership structure to asset prices.
