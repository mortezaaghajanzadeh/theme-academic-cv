I am a PhD candidate in Finance at the Swedish House of Finance, Stockholm School of Economics.

**I am on the 2027–2028 academic job market.**

My research studies how venture capital networks shape innovation, market entry, and competition. I am particularly interested in venture capital and private equity, and how they affect the growth of firms. I combine theoretical modeling with structural and empirical analysis to understand how financial relationships influence market structure and the allocation of innovative activity.

My **job market paper** develops a dynamic model of VC syndication networks, showing how the relationships that improve startup outcomes can also deter entry and soften competition.
